Language Structure Principles

This page introduces the core mechanisms behind CLP (Conceptual Language Protocol), including semantic leaps, language structure-driven processes, and model evaluation.

🔷 Structure Leap

CLP’s translation mechanism is not based on word alignment but on activating conceptual structures, forming a “semantic leap path” from source to target language.

🔷 3D Semantic Space

Concepts are organized in a three-dimensional space rather than linearly. CLP uses spatial mapping to evaluate semantic alignment and leap strength.

🔷 Model Evaluation

A multi-model scoring system (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok) evaluates translated texts for structural leaps, validating CLP’s universality and effectiveness.

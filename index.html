Welcome to CLP Proto

CLP Proto is a micro-research prototype exploring how small language models handle poetic translation — and what this reveals about structure, cognition, and the illusion of linguistic agency.

This case study focuses on four English translations of Wang Zhihuan’s Dēng Guàn Què Lóu (On the Stork Tower), generated by ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. All were run under deliberately limited compute (Intel® Core™ i5-5200U, NVIDIA 940M GPU, ~600MB model class).

Through detailed comparison, we trace how poetic symmetry and semantic intent survive — or collapse — under model constraints, and how structural emergence can mimic understanding without true cognition.
